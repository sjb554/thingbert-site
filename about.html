About Thingbert

Thingbert is a studio devoted to playful, human-centered experimentation. We explore how emerging technologies can create surprising, meaningful, and accessible experiences.

Guiding Principles

  • Curiosity: Every prototype starts with a question that invites discovery.
  • Play: Interactive moments should delight and inspire, not intimidate.
  • Transparency: We share process, mistakes, and insights so others can build on them.

Capabilities

From rapid physical prototypes to immersive audiovisual installations, we combine design, code, and storytelling to bring ideas to life.
