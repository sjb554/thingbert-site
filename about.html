About Thingbert

Thingbert is led by an actuary who loves translating dense datasets into stories leaders can use. We combine statistical modeling, pragmatic judgment, and empathetic communication.

Guiding Principles

  • Integrity: Every chart is backed by reproducible methods, explicit assumptions, and stated limitations.
  • Clarity: Accessibility matters as much as accuracy—we write so non-analysts can act with confidence.
  • Curiosity: We hunt for signal across industries, surfacing adjacent trends that challenge siloed thinking.

Capabilities

Need a quick briefing or a full analytical deep dive? Thingbert builds the story, the visuals, and the model behind it.

  • Trend Diagnostics: Time-series decomposition, cohort tracking, and leading-indicator detection.
  • Scenario Modeling: Stress tests, sensitivity workups, and what-if conversations tailored to your board.
  • Decision Support: Executive-ready summaries, annotated dashboards, and facilitation for cross-functional teams.

How We Work

Our cycles are structured but lightweight so insight never waits on bureaucracy.

  • Intake: Align on the question, context, and constraints while gathering available data and benchmarks.
  • Exploration: Rapid analysis sprints surface drivers, edge cases, and narrative arcs worth sharing.
  • Delivery: Ship interactive briefings, memos, or workshops that equip stakeholders to act.
