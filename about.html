About Thingbert

Thingbert is a studio devoted to playful, human-centered experimentation. We explore how emerging technologies can create surprising, meaningful, and accessible experiences.

Guiding Principles

  • Curiosity: Every prototype starts with a question that invites discovery.
  • Play: Interactive moments should delight and inspire, not intimidate.
  • Transparency: We share process, mistakes, and insights so others can build on them.

Capabilities

From rapid physical prototypes to immersive audiovisual installations, we combine design, code, and storytelling to bring ideas to life.

  • Immersive Audio: Spatial mixes, responsive soundscapes, and adaptive music systems.
  • Interactive Spaces: Projection, light, and tactile interfaces that react to people in real time.
  • Story Systems: Narrative engines that remix content based on emotion, movement, or biometric cues.

In the Studio

Each project is shaped by small, focused cycles that allow experimentation and community input.

  • Listening Sessions: Gather perspectives from collaborators, audiences, and field recordings.
  • Prototype Sprints: Build quick iterations that make questions tangible and testable.
  • Open Notes: Publish findings, patch notes, and prompts so others can remix the work.
